About OCEMS

Online Curriculum Enrollment and Management System

System Overview

OCEMS is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline academic curriculum management, student enrollment, and course administration for educational institutions.

Key Objectives

  • Efficient course management
  • Simplified student enrollment
  • Enhanced instructor workflow
  • Comprehensive admin control

System Roles

👨‍💼 Admin

Manage students, instructors, courses, and system settings

👨‍🎓 Student

Enroll in courses, view grades, and track academic progress

👨‍🏫 Instructor

Manage assigned courses, track student enrollment and grades
